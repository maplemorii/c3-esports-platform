Discord is the primary communication channel for C3 Esports. Linking your Discord account is **required** before you can check in to matches or receive match notifications.

If you signed up using **Continue with Discord**, your account is already linked — you can skip to [setting up your profile](/getting-started/setting-up-your-profile).

---

## How to link Discord

1. Sign in to [c3esports.com](https://c3esports.com).
2. Click your avatar in the top-right corner and go to **Profile → Edit**.
3. Scroll to the **Connected Accounts** section.
4. Click **Connect Discord**.
5. A Discord authorization window opens. Make sure you are logged into the **correct Discord account**, then click **Authorize**.
6. You are redirected back. Your Discord username now appears under Connected Accounts.

---

## Troubleshooting

### "This Discord account is already linked to another profile"

Each Discord account can only be connected to one C3 Esports account. If you see this error:

- You may have previously created an account. Try signing in with Discord at [c3esports.com/auth/signin](https://c3esports.com/auth/signin) to find it.
- If you believe this is an error, contact staff in the [C3 Esports Discord](https://discord.gg/c3esports) with your Discord username so they can investigate.

### I authorized the wrong Discord account

Contact staff via a support ticket in Discord. They can unlink the wrong account so you can connect the correct one.

### The authorization window didn't open

Make sure your browser isn't blocking pop-ups from `c3esports.com`. Allow pop-ups and try again.

## What C3 Esports can see on your Discord

When you authorize, the platform requests these Discord scopes:

| Scope | Why |
|---|---|
| `identify` | Reads your Discord username and avatar |
| `email` | Associates your Discord email with your account |

We do **not** request permission to send messages, read your DMs, or join servers on your behalf.
