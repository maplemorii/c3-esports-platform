Your player profile is your public identity on the platform. A complete profile helps team managers find you, lets staff verify your eligibility, and shows your match history to opponents.

---

## First-time profile setup

When you first log in, you'll be prompted to complete your profile at [c3esports.com/profile/setup](https://c3esports.com/profile/setup). You can also access it any time via **Profile → Edit**.

---

## Required fields

### Display name
Your in-game name or tag as it will appear throughout the platform — on your team's roster page, match pages, and standings. Choose the name you use in Rocket League.

### Epic Games username
Your Rocket League account name (Rocket League uses Epic Games accounts). This is used by staff to verify your in-game identity and by replay parsing to match scoreboard data to your profile.

<Tip>
  Your Epic Games username is visible in the Rocket League main menu under your avatar. It may differ from your display name.
</Tip>

---

## Recommended fields

### Profile photo
Upload a profile picture (JPEG or PNG, square recommended). This appears next to your name across the platform.

### Steam ID
Your 17-digit Steam ID. Used as an additional identity anchor, especially if you link Rocket League to Steam. Find yours at [steamid.io](https://steamid.io) by searching your Steam profile URL.

### Discord username
Your Discord handle (e.g. `username#0000` or the newer `@username`). Populated automatically if you signed up with Discord.

### Bio
A short description of yourself — optional, appears on your public profile page.

---

## Connecting additional game accounts

Under **Profile → Edit → Connected Accounts** you can link:

- **Discord** — required for match participation (see [Linking Discord](/getting-started/linking-discord))
- **Steam** — optional, used for identity verification
- **Epic Games** — optional OAuth link, separate from manually entering your username

## Editing your profile later

Go to [c3esports.com/profile/edit](https://c3esports.com/profile/edit) at any time to update your display name, photo, bio, or connected accounts.

<Warning>
  Changing your display name mid-season may cause confusion for opponents and staff. If you need to change it, let your team manager know.
</Warning>

## Next steps

Once your profile is set up and your `.edu` is verified, you're ready to compete:

- **Join a team** — Ask a team manager to add you to their roster, or [create your own team](/managers/creating-a-team).
- **Browse the current season** — View active divisions and standings at [c3esports.com/seasons](https://c3esports.com/seasons).
